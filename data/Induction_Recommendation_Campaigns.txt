        <div style="margin-top: 0.5em;">Basics of Recommendation Campaigns</div>
    </div>
	
	<div style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; border: 1px solid #6690bc; width: 98%; text-align: center;">
        <span style="text-align: center; line-height: 30px; color: #6690bc; font-size: 17px;">Reports Intro</span>
        <table style="margin: 0px auto; width: 94%;">
            <tbody>
                <tr>
                    <td colspan="3" style="width: 28px; text-align: left; padding-left: 30px;">
                        <p><span style="line-height: 1.5em; color: #000000; font-size: 10pt;">MaxRECOMMEND employs 'visitor affinity' model, which is a product recommendation engine used to determine, which products to recommend to a visitor from a group of relevant items by certain affinity criterion. Among the affinity criteria are the following ones:</span></p>
						
						<p><span style="line-height: 1.5em; color: #000000; font-size: 10pt;">
							<ul style='line-height: 1.5em; color: #000000; font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10px; list-style-type: none;'>
								<li>1.	'Bought This - Bought That'</li>
								<li>2.	'Viewed This Viewed That'</li>
								<li>3.	'Viewed This Bought That'</li>
								<li>4.	'Most Viewed' - 'Most Bought' ratings;</li>
								<li>5.	'Last Viewed' that uses 5 most recent ratings in a cookie;</li>
								<li>6.	'Rated This Rated That' </li>
								<li>7.	'Add Product to Basket', and some other criteria also. </li>
							</ul>
						</span></p>
						<p><span style="line-height: 1.5em; color: #000000; font-size: 10pt;">The system of collecting 'ratings' or 'preferences' for a given product is part of recommendation logic: inventory file of products is uploaded to the system to incrementally gather products ratings over a certain period of time.</span></p>
						<p><span style="line-height: 1.5em; color: #000000; font-size: 10pt;">Item affinity identifies items to present to a visitor based on models such as, 'visitors who viewed this bought that' or 'visitors who bought this also bought that'.</span></p>
						<p><span style="line-height: 1.5em; color: #000000; font-size: 10pt;">Visitor affinity looks at a visitor's product interest compared with other visitors and recommends products based on visitors with similar interests.</span></p>
			   </td>
